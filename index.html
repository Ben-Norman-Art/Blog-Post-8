Updates Knights

One aspect that was very common with the game was adding a sword and swing feature to the game in order for the players to be able to defend and attack the enemies within the game. Which me and my partner decided would be a good idea to include and move forward with it. With this it was difficult deciding how to structure the enemies in the game in order for the players to use them. A lot of feedback consisted of not having the enemy slimes not grab onto the players thus resulting in an attack action within the mechanics. Me and my partner still decided to keep the enemy slimes the way that they were due to the difficulty that proceeded within the gameplay. Overall, this was a very fun game to help create and I would have to give Brian a big thank you for the amount of time he has put up with especially during his last semester here.
